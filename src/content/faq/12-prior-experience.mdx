---
question: "What if my child has already taken coding classes before?"
order: 12
category: "general"
published: true
---

That's great! Prior coding experience is welcome but not required. Each week offers both **"Easy"** and **"Advanced"** modes, allowing students to choose more challenging options if they're feeling confident. This means experienced coders can push themselves further while beginners work at a comfortable pace—all within the same cohort.

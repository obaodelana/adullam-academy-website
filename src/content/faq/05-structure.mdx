order: 5

The learning rhythm is designed to move from foundations to building:
- **Weeks 1–2:** Building a working understanding of the core programming language and tools
- **Week 3:** Students begin applying what they learned by building apps
- **Weeks 4-6:** More complex development topics are introduced and implemented
- **Weeks 7-8:** Time is reserved to build, polish, and present the final project
